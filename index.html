About Me

Hello, I’m Chandrashekhar Saini, born in Phulera, Jaipur. I have a strong academic foundation with a Diploma in Computer Science & Engineering (2022) and a Bachelor of Arts (2023). Currently, I’m pursuing my Master’s in Computer Applications (MCA) to strengthen my technical and creative skills further. My professional journey as a Graphic Designer began in 2023, sparked by a growing interest in visual storytelling. I started with Canva, where I learned to design creatives and understand the basics of visual communication. Over time, I developed a deeper interest in video editing, which led me to explore Adobe Premiere Pro and, eventually, Adobe After Effects to enhance my motion graphics capabilities. Along the way, I’ve also picked up working knowledge of tools like Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator, which I use occasionally for design tasks such as high editing. I enjoy working on corporate-style projects, where I can apply both my design and editing skills. I’m continuously learning and evolving, and my journey from beginner tools to more advanced software reflects my curiosity, adaptability, and commitment to growth. I aim to create graphics and videos that not only look good but also communicate, connect emotionally, and deliver results.
